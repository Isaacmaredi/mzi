Brief background about the maiden book

Launch Date

15 November, 2019

The book is set out as a celebration of the often-understated achievements and contributions of the continent to the world. It starts by surveying the landscape that had numerous structures and artefacts that are abound throughout the continent that point to some systemic knowledge development that has existed from time immemorial

It further takes point with the global north being ungrateful beneficiaries of contributions made by the continent by continuously referring to it as backward, dark and illiterate.
