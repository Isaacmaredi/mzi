Mzilikazi the Great

The book is about a brave tactician who was also way ahead of his time as one of the things he initiated was a treaty with the Cape whereby he was trying to avoid conflict with the mixed breed group of the Griquas favourites of the Boers

Mhlahlandlela was an important seat of power where Mzilikazi set up his Royal palace after moving from Ntabazinduna where he first arrived in Zimbabwe. It is also important to note that Mhlahlandlela had previously been established during his migratory times when he first settled along the banks of the Enzabuhlungu (Apies) River - where Pretoria was later established.

A Book for All ...

The story in its movements touches different clans, “tribes” and nations and hopefully answers and plugs many gaps that may have been left open in the past.

This book is unique as it straddles a variety of audiences. It is relevant for schools, universities, historians and general public who may have an interest in their recent past history which has been largely presented from European viewpoint exclusively in the past.
