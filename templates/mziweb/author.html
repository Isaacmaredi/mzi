About the Author

Nthebe Molope is my name ...

  • nthebe@mzilikazithegreat.org
  • +27 0 76 928 5757

I am a fifty nine year old former teacher and consider myself a life student of African history, a subject which I am so passionate about. Mzilikazi the Great is my second offering since I starting tryin my hand in contributing to knowledge creation. The maiden book was An Evolutionary Review of Black Education and I am currently working on the third offering which could be ready by first quarter of 2024. I consider these books to be my small contribution to the global teaching world which I have not totally abandoned since I quit the classroom over thirty one years ago.

My approach in writing is inspired by reading broadly, researching and establishing what is contested narratives. I am thus happy that I sustained this principle here and covered all the countries in which Mzilikazi had been as I tracked him. To give further credence to the story a platform for weekly debates and a blog will be created where I shall interact with different people on the history of Africa.

The production of this book could not have happened or been met with so much success had it not been for the financial injection that I received from Sport and Recreation Arts and Culture (SRAC) through the Mzansi Golden Awards initiative.

There are different activities that are to be carried out to compliment the marketing and publicity of the book. These will include pre-launch activation. Advertising of launch and countdown. National and regional launch dates and venues. To keep abreast with all these activities it will be important to continue watching this space.
